Games

This past semester, I took Intro to Game Development and was finally able to make some games of my own! I'm still a beginner but I plan on improving my skills by taking other game design courses offered by NYU. Some of my favorite games include Call of Duty, Fortnite, Minecraft, and Rocket League.
